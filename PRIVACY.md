# Privacy Policy — SnackSnap

**Last updated:** March 29, 2026  

**Contact:** quangbinhdang@gmail.com

This policy describes how the **SnackSnap** iOS app (“the App”) handles information.

## Summary

- The App is designed to work **without an account**.  
- **Snack photos, captions, moods, and related content you create** are stored **on your device** using Apple’s frameworks (e.g. SwiftData).  
- We **do not** sell your personal information.  
- This version does **not** include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs as described in our public App Store materials.

If you change the App later (e.g. add accounts, cloud sync, or analytics), **update this policy and App Store Connect** before release.

## Information the App processes

### Information you provide

- **Photos** you take with the camera or choose from your photo library, when you use those features.  
- **Text** you add (e.g. captions) and **mood labels** you select.

### Information stored on device

- Your snack entries and preferences needed for the App to work are kept **on your iPhone**.  
- **App preferences** may be stored on your device (e.g. via User Defaults) to remember settings.

### Notifications

If the App offers reminders in a future update and you enable them, those may use **local notifications** on your device. The current shipping version focuses on on-device journaling without a login.

## What we do not do (current version)

- We do **not** require you to create an account to use core features.  
- We do **not** operate our own backend in the shipping build to store your snack diary.  
- We do **not** use the App to **track** you across other companies’ apps or websites for advertising (and we do not integrate third-party ad or analytics SDKs in the build described on the store).

## Apple and App Store

The App runs on **Apple’s iOS**. Apple’s own terms and privacy practices apply to the device and App Store.

## Children

The App is not directed specifically at children. If you are a parent and believe a child has provided information to us through a channel we control, contact us at the email above.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.
