# SnackSnap — Support

**SnackSnap** is an iPhone app (iOS 17+) for a playful **snack photo diary**: snap your treats, add **mood chips** and optional captions, and browse your **feed**, **week** collage, and **insights**—all **on your device**. It is **not** a calorie or nutrition tracker.

## Get help

- **Email:** [quangbinhdang@gmail.com](mailto:quangbinhdang@gmail.com)

We can help with bugs, questions about how the app works, and data on your device.

## FAQ

**Do I need an account?**  
No. SnackSnap works without signing in.

**Where is my data stored?**  
On your iPhone using Apple’s on-device storage (SwiftData). This version does not sync to our servers.

**Photos & camera**  
You can take a photo or choose from your library. Images are saved on device for your scrapbook.

**Multiple snacks in one day**  
You can log more than one snack photo per day. The feed groups them by day.

**Tracking / ads**  
We do not use third-party analytics or ad SDKs in the version described in our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).

**Delete my data**  
Delete the app to remove its on-device data.
